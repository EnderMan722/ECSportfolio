Lunacy

Lunacy is a 2D psychological horror game. You wake up in an open clearing with things shifting just out of sight. There is a single dwindling campfire in the middle. You have to survive each night and rekindle/revive the campfire before it runs out. Gather wood and leaves to restock it before you lose your sanity. Survive long enough and send out an S.O.S. to escape! Don't take too long before things come for you...

Key features include:
  • Uneasy feelings and shifting
  • A fun shooting mini game
  • Puzzle solving and figuring out what is going on
  • Visuals and audio designed to evoke the game with a pixelated feel

    Just Jump

    Race through a series of 2D levels jumping and wall jumping with only one button. The game starts out in a grassy plain to teach you how to play, then moves on to a really hard lava biome near the final stages of the game. There is also going to be a final boss. It will have a timer and if it runs out you lose, so you have to collect time buffs around the map to survive and keep playing the game.

    Key features include:
  • One Key Gameplay
  • Increasingly hard gameplay
  • Final Boss
  • Original art
